Atrahasis' Deluge I (Tablet II)

The People Grew Too Numerous

  • OBV i

    1. 6oo years, less than 6oo, passed

    2. And the country became too wide, the people too numerous.

    3. The God grew restless at their clamour,

    4. Ellil had to listen to their noise.

    5. He addressed the great gods,

    6. 'The noise of mankind has become too much.

    7. I am losing sleep over their racket.

      8. Call for Catastrophe

    8. Cut off food supplies to the people!

    9. Let the vegetation be too scant for their hunger

    10. Let Adad wipe away his rain.

    11. Below let no flood-water flow from the springs.

    12. Let wind go, let it strip the ground bare,

    13. Let clouds gather but not drop rain,

    14. Let the field yield a diminished harvest,

    15. Let Nissaba stop up her bosom.

    16. No happiness shall come to them.

    17. Let their [ ] be dejected.

      18. Call for Response by Enki for Atrahasis

    18. Call the elders, the senior men

    19. Start an uprising in your house

    20. Let heralds proclaim ...

    21. Let them make a loud noise in the land:

    22. Do not revere your gods!

    23. Do not pray to your goddess!

    24. Search out the door of Adad,

    25. Bring a baked loaf into his presence.

    26. May the flour offering reach him,

    27. May he be shamed by the presents

    28. And wipe away his "hand".
